Research

The Business Growth DNA™ assessment is the result of an extensive research project that identified the critical personal factors contributing to growth for owner-managers of businesses with a turnover of between £0.5m and £20m

The Business Growth DNA™ research project team reviewed the extensive academic literature on entrepreneurial success factors. A qualitative research phase was then undertaken, in the form of interviews with owner-managers who have successfully grown their businesses to further refine a series of critical growth factors. The final research stage involved extensive statistical testing with a large sample of UK owner-managers, to ensure that the assessment is concise, psychometrically robust, and benchmarked against the relevant population.

What is Business Growth DNA™

Based on research into the traits of successful UK entrepreneurs by the Business Growth Programme team at Cranfield School of Management, Business Growth DNA™ is an assessment developed on psychometric principles, that identifies the key areas of personal development that you need to focus on in order to take your business to the next level.

How quickly will I get my profile report?

Instantly! Once the assessment is finished we will send the report in PDF format to your email account. Our algorithms automatically compile your report based on tens of thousands of possible permutations.

Can I retake the assessment?

Yes. However, to accurately assess any changes in your performance against the critical factors, we recommend that you re-take the assessment at 12 monthly intervals. We intend to launch an option to enable you to compare your results and will notify you when it’s available.

How can I improve performance against the Business Growth DNA™ factors?

We would suggest that you consider Cranfield’s Business Growth Programme (BGP), the UK’s longest running and most successful programme for the development of owner-managers. Over the past 27 years, many of our delegates have gone on to become multi-millionaires. Designed specifically for the development of owner-managers, the Business Growth Programme helps you create the future you want for your business and for yourself.

This programme provides a unique opportunity for you to step back from the day-to-day demands of running your business. By the end of the programme you will have developed a comprehensive and robust strategy and plan for the future.
